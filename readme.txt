= English =
* Displays the weather infortmation for Turkish cities.
* Ability to choose the city


You can see the working demo here [Weather in Turkey](http://www.iwasinturkey.com/weather-in-turkey "Weather in Turkey")  and download here [WordPress Plugins](http:lycie.com "WordPress Plugins")

= Turkce =
* Gunluk hava durumunu gosterir
* Istenilen sehri secme olanagi var

Demo versiyon -> [Hava Durumu](http://www.iwasinturkey.com/weather-in-turkey  "Hava Durumu")  and indirmek icin -> [WordPress Eklentileri](http://lycie.com "WordPress Eklentileri")


== Installation ==

* Download 'weatherinturkey.zip' to your computer and unzip it.
* Upload `weatherinturkey` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
* Once you upload the plugin activate it through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
* Finally, add the widget to your sidebar through the 'Appearance > Wigdets' menu
